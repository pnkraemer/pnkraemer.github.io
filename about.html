About me

My name is Nicholas Krämer. I am an MSc student of mathematics at the University of Bonn and a part-time research assistant at the Institute for Numerical Simulation, working on meshfree methods and scattered data approximation. I am currently writing up my MSc dissertation in which I investigate questions related to Gaussian process emulators in Bayesian inverse problems.
