Nico Krämer is a postdoc in the Section for Cognitive Systems at Denmark's Technical University, working with Søren Hauberg. He earned his PhD from the University of Tübingen under Philipp Hennig's supervision and holds an MSc in Mathematics from the University of Bonn. Nico's research focuses on numerical methods in machine learning, including probabilistic numerics, differentiable programming, and uncertainty quantification.
