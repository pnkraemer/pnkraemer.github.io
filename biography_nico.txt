Nico Krämer is a postdoc in Søren Hauberg's group at the Section for Cognitive Systems at Denmark's Technical University. He is an affiliated researcher at the Pioneer Centre for AI, and a member of the ELLIS society.
Nico received a PhD from the University of Tübingen, supervised by Philipp Hennig, and holds an MSc in Mathematics from the University of Bonn. 

His research revolves around finding better ways to use numerical methods in machine learning, focusing on probabilistic numerics, differentiable programming, and uncertainty quantification.
