Hi! I am Nico, and a PhD student in Machine Learning at the University of Tübingen supervised by Prof. Dr. Philipp Hennig. I am currently working on probabilistic numerics and the simulation of differential equations. Prior to my PhD, I was a student research assistant at the Institute for Numerical Simulation at the University of Bonn. I hold an MSc in Mathematics from the University of Bonn and a BSc in Mathematics in Business and Economics from the University of Mannheim. Find a full CV on the media page!
