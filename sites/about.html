Peter Nicholas Krämer
PhD Student Machine Learning // University of Tübingen

I am a PhD student in Machine Learning at the University of Tübingen supervised by Prof. Dr. Philipp Hennig. I am currently working on probabilistic numerics and the simulation of differential equations. Prior to my PhD, I was a student research assistant at the Institute for Numerical Simulation at the University of Bonn. I hold an MSc in Mathematics from the University of Bonn and a BSc in Mathematics in Business and Economics from the University of Mannheim.

Short CV

  • PhD in Machine Learning, University of Tübingen (since 2019)
  • MSc in Mathematics, University of Bonn (2019)
  • BSc in Mathematics in Business and Economics, University of Mannheim (2016)
  • Higher education entrance qualification, Carl-Fuhlrott-Gymnasium Wuppertal (2013)
