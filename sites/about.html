Peter Nicholas Krämer
PhD Student Machine Learning // University of Tübingen

Since September 2019, I have been a PhD student and research assistant in Machine Learning at the University of Tübingen under supervision of Prof. Dr. Philipp Hennig. I am working on data-driven inference and probabilistic solvers for all kinds differential equations. Prior to this, I was a student research assistant at the Institute for Numerical Simulation at the University of Bonn. I hold an MSc in Mathematics from the University of Bonn and a BSc in Mathematics in Business and Economics from the University of Mannheim.

  • MSc in Mathematics, University of Bonn (2019)
