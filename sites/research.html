Peter Nicholas Krämer
PhD Student Machine Learning // University of Tübingen

I am working on probabilistic numerics for differential equations and related topics. This relates to automated, data-driven inference for mechanical models as part of the ADIMEM project (see here) and utilises theory from the areas of

  • Bayesian filtering and smoothing
  • Meshfree approximation
and (Bayesian) computational statistics in general.

At the Institute for Numerical Simulation in Bonn, I have been working on numerical linear algebra for linear systems involving kernel matrices and scattered data approximation in general. On the media page, you can find an abstract of my MSc thesis. The code is on GitHub: see here.

If you share any of these interests, please feel free to get in touch!
